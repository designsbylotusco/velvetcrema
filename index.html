Our Story

Where Good Coffee Meets Good Company

Nestled in the heart of Toronto's waterfront, Velvet Crema is more than just a coffee shop—it's a sanctuary for those who appreciate the finer, slower moments in life.

We believe great coffee shouldn't come with a great price tag. Our carefully curated menu offers everything from perfectly pulled espressos to creamy lattes, all at prices that let you come back again and again.

Whether you're catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or finding a quiet corner to work, Velvet Crema welcomes you to stay as long as you'd like.
